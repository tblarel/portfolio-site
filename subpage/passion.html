Team Lineup Animation


Being a huge fan of Soccer and Statistics, I spent some time working on a concept page for match recaps & statistics. I began by creating assets for the soccer pitch, players, and formations in Photoshop.


I next animated these in Adobe AfterEffects before exporting the elements & implementing them with on the web with the Lottie.js library. The result is a smooth and responsive animation. Below is a video demonstrating the result running on a webpage.


Parallax Animation


A practice in using parralax to bring a 2D image to life. I began with a still image and seperated the various layers and fixed the background in Photoshop as shown in the progress gif. Next, I imported the layer elements to AfterEffects. Placing and animating these layers and a 3D camera give the illusion of motion and depth to the scene. Lastly I added in some subtle grass animated with Trapcode Particular.

Alternative Scoreboard Design


The last small World Cup project I worked on was an alternative scoreboard. I found the scoreboards used by the cable networks to be cluttered and not very aesthetically pleasing. I designed my own scoreboard inspired by Google's 'Material Design' flat look and tried to make it as readable as possible while including small details like the country's respective kit colors beneath their names..
