In a similar vein to Photography, I have long been drawn to film and videos, particularly the means in which videos are edited and animated. Above are a selection of videos I have edited over the years in Adobe AfterEffects.

Through these projects I have taught myself and gained experience color correcting, syncing, masking, and animating. In addition, I taught myself Industry tools such as Boujou for 3D motion tracking and Trapcode Particular for 3D animation and rendering of particles.
