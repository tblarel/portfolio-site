Student Support

Beginning my junior year and continuing through my senior year, I was employed by the University of Miami IT department as a Student Web Designer.

My major assignment was to lead the redesign of the student support website. Working with JavaScript,HTML, CSS, and the Foundation framework , I rewrote the site to match the style and aesthetics of the University's other websites, ensuring that the site would also be responsive to different device sizes.
