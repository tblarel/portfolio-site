Masters of Real Estate & Urbanism

During my Senior year, I worked part-time as a Web & Graphic Designer for the School of Architecture, specifically the Masters program in Real Estate Development & Urbanism (MRED+U).

One of my first major projects was to lead the redesign of the program's website. I began by working with the Masters program coordinator to create a new sitemap in Slickplan. I then used the University's enterprise Cascade CMS system to implement the new site and used photos I had captured around campus for images on various site pages.
