    • Sonify | Full Stack

    ×

    Sonify was developed as a Capstone Senior Design Project by a team of three engineering students at the University of Miami. The Full Stack Web Application provides recommendations based off of the acoustic properties of a given track. By interfacing with Spotify's API, User's can search for recommendations of any track in Spotify's catalog. The track's audio file is then analyzed on the Back End where 12 features such as tempo and chord progressions are extracted. We applied machine learning to provide recommendations, feeding our feature vectror into a Weighted KNN algorithm and using a Wave-Hedges distance metric to recommend the 10 nearest neighbors. By making AJAX calls to Spotify's API and fetching album art and audio previews, the results are then presented on the Front End to the user.
